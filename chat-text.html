The 2017 BMW X4: A Sports Activity Coupe That Stands Out

The BMW X4 has always been a unique entry into the luxury SUV market, positioning itself as a **sports activity coupe**. In 2017, the BMW X4 continued to impress with its blend of dynamic performance, distinctive styling, and luxurious features. This year marked a period where two generations of the X4 were available, allowing buyers to explore both the newer model's enhancements and the tried-and-tested features of the earlier iteration.

Distinctive Design

The exterior design of the 2017 BMW X4 embodies a striking balance between muscularity and elegance. With its **sloping roofline** and pronounced wheel arches, the X4 presents a sporty yet sophisticated aesthetic. The signature BMW kidney grille sits prominently at the front, flanked by sharp, **sweeping headlights** that give the vehicle an assertive presence on the road.

The coupe-like silhouette is accentuated by the rear-end design, featuring LED taillights and a sculpted bumper. This design not only enhances aerodynamics but also contributes to its sporty profile. The X4's **wheelbase** is slightly longer than that of the X3, providing a more stable and planted feel during drives. Overall, the 2017 BMW X4 is an eye-catching vehicle that stands apart from traditional SUVs.

Luxurious Interior

Inside, the 2017 X4 welcomes occupants with a driver-focused cockpit and high-quality materials that exemplify BMW's commitment to luxury. The dashboard features **premium finishes**, and the layout synthesizes functionality with modern aesthetics. With leather upholstery, soft-touch surfaces, and wood accents, the interior creates an inviting ambiance.

  • Space for up to five passengers
  • Comfortable front and rear seating with ample legroom

The **infotainment system** is operated via BMW's iDrive system, integrated with a 6.5-inch display, with an optional upgrade to a larger 8.8-inch touchscreen. Connectivity features include Bluetooth, USB ports, and compatibility with apps, ensuring that drivers remain connected while on the go.

"The interior of the BMW X4 is designed for those who appreciate luxury and performance in equal measure."

Technical Specifications

Underpinning the sporty demeanor of the X4 is its robust technical foundation. In 2017, the X4 was available with a choice of rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive (BMW's xDrive). The suspension setup offers a comfortable ride while maintaining precise handling characteristics, making it suitable for both city and spirited country driving.

Engines and Modifications

The engine lineup for the 2017 BMW X4 varies, accommodating a range of driving preferences. The base model is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine, generating 240 horsepower. For those seeking more power, the available 3.0-liter inline-six engine produces an impressive 335 horsepower.

  1. X4 xDrive28i: 2.0-liter twin-scroll turbocharged engine
  2. X4 xDrive35i: 3.0-liter inline-six turbocharged engine

Both engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, delivering smooth shifts and responsive acceleration. The **fuel efficiency** is commendable, with the X4 xDrive28i rated at approximately 20 mpg city and 28 mpg highway.

Trim Levels

The 2017 X4 was offered in several trim levels, reflecting varying features and performance enhancements. The trim levels include:

  • X4 xDrive28i: Base model with a comprehensive suite of features
  • X4 xDrive35i: Enhanced performance and additional luxury amenities

Each trim level comes with its own set of options, allowing buyers to customize their X4 according to personal preferences. Notable options include **premium sound systems**, advanced safety features, and upgraded navigation systems.

Impressive Handling

What sets the 2017 BMW X4 apart from competitors is its **exceptional handling** characteristics. The combination of a low center of gravity and well-tuned suspension provides a driving experience that feels sporty and athletic.

Whether navigating tight corners or cruising along the highway, the X4 delivers a responsive feel with minimal body roll. This is further enhanced by the optional Dynamic Handling Package, which boasts features such as Adaptive Suspension and Dynamic Stability Control.
